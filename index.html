✦ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before ordering.

Can I use the delivered files commercially?
Yes. Standard and Premium packages include commercial use licenses. Basic packages are for personal and church use. For large-scale commercial projects (TV broadcast, millions of streams), please contact us for a custom quote.
How do I make payment?
We accept all Indian payment methods: UPI, Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, NEFT and IMPS. For Basic and Standard packages, full payment is required before we start. For Premium packages, 50% advance is sufficient to begin. We share our UPI ID / bank details on WhatsApp.
What if I'm not satisfied with the result?
We offer revisions on all packages (1 for Basic, 2 for Standard, unlimited for Premium). If after all revisions you are still not satisfied, we offer a partial refund on a case-by-case basis. We always aim for 100% client satisfaction.
How quickly will I receive my order?
Basic and Standard orders are delivered within 24–48 hours of payment and receiving your requirements. Premium orders take 3–5 working days. We always confirm the exact delivery time when your order is placed.
Do you serve clients outside India?
Yes! We are based in India but serve clients worldwide. International clients can pay via Wise, Payoneer, or contact us for alternatives. We have clients across India, USA, Canada, UK, UAE and more.
How do I send my requirements?
Simply WhatsApp us at +91 86673 65225. Tell us the package you want, any theme, style, Bible verse or reference you have in mind. The more details you share, the better the result. We typically respond within 1–2 hours (Mon–Sat, 9AM–9PM IST).
